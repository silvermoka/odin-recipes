Red Sauce

Description

A delicious, hearty, meat-based red sauce to serve with your favorite pasta.

Ingredients

  • Pork neck bones (or other pork bone-in meat for flavor)
  • Garlic--a lot!
  • 1/2 Sweet onion
  • 1/2 cup red wine
  • 8oz chicken stock
  • 2 28oz cans La Valle DOP whole peeled tomatoes
  • 1 28oz can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can of tomato paste
  • Red pepper flakes
  • A lot of fresh basil
  • Oregano
  • Pepper
  • Kosher salt
  • Olive oil for browning and sauteing

Steps

  1. Brown the pork, set aside. Add olive oil to same pan with pork drippings, caramelize onion in it.

  2. Saute garlic, then add garlic and onion to main pot. Add chicken stock, red wine, red pepper flakes, oregano, some of the basil, and add pork back in. Once heated, add tomato paste and let dissolve. Simmer for 1 hour.

  3. Turn the heat up and add the crushed tomato, and either hand-crush or process the peeled tomatoes to desired texture and add to pot.

  4. Add a little more salt and spices, stir, and bring the heat back down to a simmer for the remainder of the cooking time. Cover with a lid for the first half, remove the lid for the second half. 4-6 hours is the minimum, but you may keep it going for longer.

  5. If desired, you may brown and add italian sausage, meatballs, or braciole to the sauce to cook.
