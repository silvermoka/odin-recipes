Garlic Bread

Description

A delicious side dish of garlic bread that goes great with braciole and red sauce!

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf of french bread
  • Minced garlic
  • 1/2 cup of softened butter
  • Minced parsley
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Slice french bread loaf into slices, but not all the way through, and place on a foil covered baking sheet.

  2. In a bowl, combine all other ingredients until thoroughly mixed. Using a butter knife, spread the mixture between each slice and sprinkle parsley and salt on top.

  3. Bake for 8-10 minutes, either covering with foil or leaving uncovered for desired texture.

  4. Slice or pull the slices apart and serve.
