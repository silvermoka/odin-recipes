Braciole

Description

Tender beef rolled up and stuffed with parmesan and basil, and stewed for hours in a hearty red sauce.

Ingredients

  • Flank steak, or any flat cut of beef that can be cut thin and rolled up
  • Breadscrumbs
  • Parmesan
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh basil
  • Garlic in any form
  • Cheese such as mozzarella or provolone in slices
  • Butcher's twine

    • Optional:

  • A seasoning blend like Tony's or Cavender's
  • Proscuitto or roasted eggplant

Steps

  1. After the meat is cut into thin slices, cover the meat with a piece of plastic and use a mallet to tenderize and thin it out.

  2. Mix breadcrumbs, parmesan, salt and pepper, and other spices in a bowl. Add olive oil until it has a paste consistency.

  3. Spread a thin layer of the mixture onto a piece of the beef, then add a basil leaf, cheese, and other preferred ingredients.Roll up the meat and tie off securely with butcher's twine at each end.

  4. Brown each side in olive oil, then cook the braciole in a red sauce until tender.
